Graffiti

An infrastructure for building social software.

Graffiti is a system that lets you build a wide variety of custom social apps with using only front end code—no need to build or deploy your own server. You can build apps like Twitter, Messenger, Wikipedia or something completely new!

Applications within the Graffiti ecosystem also interoperate. You can freely move between Graffiti apps—each with its own aesthetic, feature set, and moderation—all without losing your friends or data!
